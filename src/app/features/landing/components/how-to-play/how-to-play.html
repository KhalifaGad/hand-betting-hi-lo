How to Play

Read the hand. Call the next one.

Each hand of Mahjong tiles has a total value. Bet whether the next hand will total higher or lower. String correct calls into a streak, dodge the bust, and beat your personal best.

  1. 1

    Read your hand

    Every tile has a value. Their sum is the hand total you're betting against.

  2. 2

    Call higher or lower

    Predict where the next hand's total lands versus the current one.

    ▼ Lower ▲ Higher
  3. 3

    Reveal & resolve

    Right call → score and grow your streak. Wrong call → the streak resets.
